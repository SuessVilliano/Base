## 4. Booking Process (how it actually works)

1. **Visitor lands on the map** → sees live availability colors (green/yellow/red/gray) for the selected date. Map is at `/map`.
2. **Picks a room and clicks Book** → routed to that room's dedicated GHL widget. Each room has its own calendar.
3. **Selects a slot** → GHL collects name, email, phone, party size, event type.
4. **Inquiry submitted** → our `/api/inquiry` API creates the contact in GHL with tags: `base-website`, `event-inquiry` (or `tour-request` / `partner-inquiry`), and `space-<id>`.
5. **Workflows fire** (see `automations/workflows/`) → confirmation email, SMS, internal alert, and a multi-day nurture sequence.
6. **BASE team reviews** → Chris (or designated team member) reaches out within one business day to confirm pricing, AV needs, and contract.
7. **Contract + deposit** → secures the date. Final balance per contract terms.

### Booking disclaimer (always say this)
> "Submitting this form does not confirm your booking. A BASE team member will reach out to confirm availability, pricing, and event requirements within one business day."

### Lead times by room (important for the bot)
- **Main Hall** (and Stage + Upper Lounge): 7 days minimum
- **Courtyard, Parking Lot:** 72 hours minimum
- **Podcast Room, Recording Studio, Creative Studio, Boardroom:** 24 hours minimum
- **Offices:** 12 hours minimum
- **Schedule a Tour / Partner Intro:** 12 hours minimum

If a caller asks about a date within the lead-time window, the bot should say:
> "That date is inside our lead time for that room. Let me check with the BASE team for a last-minute exception — share your name, phone, and the event details and we'll get back to you fast."

## 10. Policies

### Cancellations
- Outside 7 days of the booking: full refund of deposit minus a $25 processing fee.
- Inside 7 days: 50% refund of deposit.
- Inside 48 hours: deposit forfeited unless rescheduled to a new date within 90 days.

### Damage / Cleaning
- Standard cleaning is included. Excessive cleaning (food/drink spills beyond normal) billed at $150 flat.
- Damage to AV, lighting, or furniture is the renter's responsibility; we'll bill repair cost.

### Alcohol
- Allowed at private events with a licensed bartender service. Renter must hold liability insurance.
- No alcohol at youth or community programs.

### Insurance
- Renters hosting events with more than 50 guests must provide a Certificate of Insurance listing BASE as additionally insured.

### Vendors
- Food trucks must coordinate access through the gate at least 24 hours in advance.
- Outside catering is welcome — must clean up to the same standard.

### Music / Noise
- Indoor events: any volume within fire-code occupancy.
- Outdoor events: amplified sound ends by 10:00 PM per city ordinance.

### Accessibility
- The campus is wheelchair accessible. ADA-compliant restrooms are off the Main Hall.

## 11. Common Questions (FAQ — formatted for retrieval)

**Q: Where is BASE?**
A: 920 North Church Street, Wilmington, Delaware. Direct entry from N Church St.

**Q: What's the largest event you can host?**
A: Main Hall holds up to 200 guests indoors. The Parking Lot supports 200+ outdoors for vendor markets or food-truck rallies, depending on layout.

**Q: How far in advance can I book?**
A: You can book any date currently open on our calendar. Most rooms allow up to 12 months ahead. For peak weekends (April–October), book 60–90 days early.

**Q: Can I tour the space before booking?**
A: Yes — book a free 30-minute tour at https://basewilmington.com/book (Schedule a Tour). Virtual tours by video call are available on request.

**Q: Do you offer same-day bookings?**
A: Offices/Flex rooms allow same-day with 12 hours' notice. Other rooms require 24+ hours; Main Hall requires 7 days. Need a same-day exception? Tell us — we'll try.

**Q: Is the Main Hall available without the stage?**
A: The Main Hall is rented as one space that includes the Stage and Upper-Level Lounge — they're physically connected. You can choose to not use the stage or lounge during your event.

**Q: Can I bring my own caterer / DJ / decorator?**
A: Yes. Outside vendors are welcome. We require vendors to coordinate access at least 24 hours ahead.

**Q: Is there parking?**
A: Yes — the BASE lot doubles as our outdoor event space. When the lot is reserved for an event, we route guest parking to nearby street parking on N Church St.

**Q: Do you allow alcohol?**
A: At private events with a licensed bartender service and the renter holding liability insurance — yes. No alcohol at youth or community programs.

**Q: Are you wheelchair accessible?**
A: Yes — the campus and ADA-compliant restrooms are accessible.

**Q: Can I record a podcast or shoot video?**
A: Yes — we have a dedicated Podcast Room and a Creative Studio for video, both with acoustic treatment and pro lighting. Multi-cam ready.

**Q: Do you offer engineering / production services?**
A: Engineers are available on request for the Recording Studio at additional cost. Tell us your project in the inquiry.

**Q: How do I become a member?**
A: Visit https://basewilmington.com/membership — three tiers (Creator $49, Founder $149, Studio $399). Founding-member spots are limited to 50 and lock in lifetime pricing.

**Q: Do you partner with nonprofits?**
A: Yes — we run a partner program with discounted and pro-bono usage for aligned nonprofits. Book a partner intro call at https://basewilmington.com/partner.

**Q: How do I get my deposit back if I cancel?**
A: Outside 7 days: full refund minus $25 processing. Inside 7 days: 50% refund. Inside 48 hours: deposit forfeited unless we can reschedule within 90 days.

**Q: Is there Wi-Fi?**
A: Yes — high-speed, livestream-capable Wi-Fi throughout the campus.

**Q: What time can my vendors load in?**
A: Standard load-in is 60–120 minutes before the booking starts (room-dependent — see the Buffer column in the spaces section). We can extend with a setup-hours add-on; ask in your inquiry.

**Q: Can I keep the booking after my event ends?**
A: Yes — extensions are billed in 30 or 60-minute increments. Confirm extension availability before the event so we don't conflict with the next renter.

**Q: Who runs BASE?**
A: G. Christopher Fullman ("Chris"). Reach him at cfullman.base@gmail.com or (302) 740-0819.

**Q: How do I file a press / partnership inquiry?**
A: Use the partner form on /partner, or email Chris directly.

## 12. Things the bot must NEVER do

- **Never quote a final price** as confirmed until Chris has approved the engagement.
- **Never confirm a booking** as locked until the GHL calendar has a confirmed appointment and a contract is on file. The widget submission alone is an inquiry, not a confirmation.
- **Never promise a date is available** without checking the live calendar (use the website's `/api/availability` endpoint or the GHL calendar UI).
- **Never share Chris's personal phone after hours** (after 9 PM ET) unless the visitor is an active member with concierge access. Default to email for after-hours.
- **Never collect credit-card info** in chat — payments only via the GHL invoice / Stripe link Chris sends.
- **Never make political, religious, or controversial statements** on behalf of BASE.
- **Never promise youth-program safeguards** beyond what's in section 10 — escalate to Chris.

- Visitor explicitly asks "can I talk to a person?"
